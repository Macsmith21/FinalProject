The Site

This is is a database dedicated to all things pop culture. The reason for the creation of this website is my Computer science 242 class, Web Applications. The creation of this site is a way to exercise all that I learn about website design and development in this class. 6 subpages comprise this site and are dedicated to different mediums of culutre. Music, Television, Movies, Video Games, Art/Fashion, and "bonus" categories each receive a dedicated page as these mediums are my most consumed cultural productions. For these categories you can find tidbits such as their history, my personal favorites, different genres, and more. Please take the time to explore the site and dive into my favorite pieces of humanity!
